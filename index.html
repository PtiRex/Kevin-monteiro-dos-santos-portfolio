Escape the fear

Escape the fear is the first project I did at school to learn programming, 2D tools and Game design rules.

Synopsis :

you play a child lost in the woods and he is afraid of the dark and you have to help him get out of the forest before the child is absorbed by the darkness

what did I do?

I did level design but also programming and 2D assets, in this project I made it alone.

This game is a die and retry, so I wanted to give the player several ways to venture into the level while advancing in the game.

A Taton

A Tatons is the final project of my second year I did at school, i mainly did the level design, game design and some 3D assets.

Synopsis :

You play one's again a kid that is affraid of the dark and you have wake up your parents.before the darkness get you

what did I do?

I did the level design but also 3D assets and lighting and in this project we were five

In Details

In the first room, the player will have to venture into the first room while learning the mechanics of the game

Then after solving the first puzzle in the first room he will have to use all the mechanics of the first room to be able to advance so he can learn a new mechanic.

After solving the second mechanism, the player will enter a room with no turning back and will have to move forward blindly

And finally the player enters the last room and will have to use all the mechanics he has learned as he has played

Robbers in disguised (on going)

Robbers in disguise is the final project of my study. Made in a team of five my collaborators were

Pitch :

Put on your costume and go on a heist in unusual places with your friends! Progress through puzzles, traps and unexpected situations using coordination and dexterity to complete your mission!

what did I do?

I worked on the level design of the game and the challenge and increasing difficulty in the game

In Details

Here is the base of the players, the place where they can do whatever they want including learning the keys and getting used to them or even hitting each other

Then, the players will have to cooperate to reach their goal and protect it throughout their journey.

After the previous room, players will have to use different mechanics while cooperating to continue in the room and move to the next room

In this room, players will have to save their objective before it dies

And finally, the players will have to face the boss of the game for that the players will use all the mechanics that they learned during their adventure to finish the level
